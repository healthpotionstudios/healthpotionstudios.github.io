Description

Out of Control is a platformer where the controls randomize each level!
Play through 30+ levels and fight challenging bosses in a game that makes you say 'Which one is jump again?'

Features

  • Hand drawn art
  • Frustrating but satisfying gameplay
  • More than 40 levels each with a collectible
  • Secret exits
  • More than 20 unique enemy types
  • Challenging bosses to test your skills

Story

Professor Croak never intended to cause problems, but coding isn’t easy when you only have three fingers. He wanted to create robots to protect the forest, but instead they malfunctioned and are on a rampage. Armed with the professor’s newest creation, it’s up to Roscoe to stop the robots and bring back peace to the forest.

Meet the Cast

Roscoe


A fun loving raccoon always looking for an adventure. He’s here to help Professor Croak deal with the robots and restore his home.

Professor Croak


Poor coding and unreliable parts made Professor Croak’s robots malfunction. He requested Roscoe’s assistance to deal with the robots and gave him a suit to help combat them. Having completely run out of parts, Roscoe’s suit is made mostly of cardboard and tape. Its random controls an unfortunate side-effect.

The baddies


Not really bad in any way. Cardboard just happens to be weak to standard woodland creatures.

Although technically none of them pay taxes.
