Description

Out of Control is a platformer where the controls randomize each level!
Play through a bunch of levels and fight challenging bosses in a game that makes you say ‘Which one is jump again?’

Features

  • Hand drawn art
  • Frustrating but satisfying gameplay
  • More than 30 levels
  • Challenging bosses

Story

Professor Croak never intended to cause problems. He wanted to create robots to protect the forest not hurt it. Unfortunately, due to poor coding, his robots are rampaging. It is now up to Roscoe (with help from Prof. Croak) to stop the robots and bring back peace to the forest.

Meet the Cast

Roscoe


A fun loving raccoon always looking for an adventure. He was able to help Professor Croak deal with the robots.

Professor Croak


Poor coding and a lack of parts made Professor Croak’s robots malfunction. He requested Roscoe’s assistance to deal with the robots and gave him a suit to help combat them. Having completely run out of parts, Roscoe’s suit is made of cardboard. Its random controls weren’t intentional.

The baddies


Not really bad in any way. Cardboard just happens to be weak to standard woodland creatures.

Although technically none of them pay taxes
